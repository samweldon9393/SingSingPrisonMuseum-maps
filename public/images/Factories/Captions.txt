Commercial products made at Sing Sing in the 19th century included hats, shoes, chains, stoves, carpets, cabinets, saddlery hardware and saws. 

Newspaper illustration, date unknown. Private collection

In the 20th century, the prison turned out products like mattresses, shoes and soap for the corrections system and other State entities. Today, Sing Sing is the only correctional facility in New York where goods are not manufactured. 

 (shoes) Photograph, 1935. Private collection
